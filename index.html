Common Questions

How much does a project cost?

Every project is unique. Pricing depends on complexity, features, and timeline. Contact me for a precise quote.

What is the typical timeline?

For standard websites ("Consult today, launch in 48h"), we move fast. Complex custom platforms typically take 2-4 weeks. Branding projects usually take 1-2 weeks depending on feedback loops.

Do you provide hosting & maintenance?

Yes. We offer monthly maintenance packages that include secure hosting, regular updates, backups, and 24/7 monitoring to ensure your digital presence never sleeps.

Can you redesign an existing site?

Absolutely. We specialize in taking outdated platforms and modernizing the stack, improving speed, and refining the UX/UI.

Do you work internationally?

Yes, while we are based in Romania, we work with clients globally. We use tools like Discord, Slack, and Zoom to maintain seamless communication regardless of timezone.
